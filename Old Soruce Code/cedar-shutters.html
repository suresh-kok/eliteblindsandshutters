Cedar Shutters

Cedar Plantation Shutters!

5 year extensive warranty!

Our Cedar Plantation Shutters are made from world-renowned Western Red Cedar (Thuja plicata) wood and come with a 5-year warranty as standard.

The mellow amber, red cinnamon and rich sienna brown colourings displayed by the wood make it a very visually attractive shutter material, and its appeal is further enhanced by the natural aromas it delicately exudes.

Western Red Cedar is a lightweight and stable wood possessing a consistent density and straight grain along its length. It lies flat and straight reducing the chances of swelling, twisting or cracking and copes incredibly well in moist or humid climates.

These are the reasons why Western Red Cedar is the timber of choice for our Cedar Plantation Shutters and Cedar Venetian Blinds.

Shutters are available both with Tiltrod in the front centre position or hidden on the back and at the side (clearview).

Please call our Prospect showroom to arrange a Cedar Plantation Shutter viewing appointment on 1300 892 674. Expected delivery time for Cedar Plantation Shutters is 6-8 weeks.
