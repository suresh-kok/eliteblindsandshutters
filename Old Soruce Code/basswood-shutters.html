BASSWOOD SHUTTERS

Basswood Shutter Specification Overview

5 year extensive warranty!

With Basswood Shutters you will experience the benefits of having the highest quality wood shutter, made from 100% furniture grade Basswood in your home. Basswood is the most durable and lightest weight hardwood available. Due to its incredible low resin content, Basswood is a stable timber suitable for use in shutters.

Available with Tiltrod front centre or alternatively with Tiltrod hidden on the back on the side (clearview).
