ABOUT US

ELITE Blinds & Shutters offers quality window coverings including Basswood timber and solid Western Red Cedar Plantation Shutters, Quality PVC Shutters as well as a range for Basswood and Cedar timber Venetians.

Our staff are highly experienced in the window coverings industry and take pride in their expertise and honest advice.

Our staff will help you make an educated choice for the right product and save you money at the same time.

Use our factory direct connection to get the low price you want and the reliability you need.

Our mission is simple, to provide a range of good quality, inexpensive window blinds and shutters in Australia whilst giving our customers outstanding customer service.

Orders are delivered with a minimum charge throughout Australia or can be installed at affordable prices in the Sydney area.
